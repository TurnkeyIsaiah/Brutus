tokens are the unit of AI compute that powers brutus's coaching. every word transcribed, every piece of feedback generated, every post-call analysis — it all costs tokens. one full coaching session (real-time + analysis) uses roughly 40,000 tokens.

a "session" depends on call length and how much real-time feedback fires. shorter calls use fewer tokens. the 1,000,000 free tokens you get on signup will comfortably cover your first few calls so you can see exactly what brutus does before spending a cent.
