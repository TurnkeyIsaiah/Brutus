we built the coach we wished we had

brutus was born from frustration. frustration with sales managers who only review calls after you've already lost the deal. frustration with "coaching" that's just criticism without actionable advice. frustration with being told what went wrong instead of being helped in real-time.

the problem

traditional sales coaching is broken. you sit through a call, make mistakes you don't even notice, lose the deal, and then—days later—someone tells you what you did wrong. by then it's too late. the prospect is gone. the damage is done.

sales managers are stretched too thin to listen to every call. and even when they do review your calls, they're reviewing history. they can't help you in the moment when you actually need it.

why brutus?

we named our AI coach after marcus junius brutus—a man famous for delivering hard truths, even when they were uncomfortable to hear. (also, yes, the stabbing thing. but mostly the hard truths part.)

brutus doesn't care about your feelings. it cares about your results. it watches your calls, identifies your mistakes in real-time, and tells you exactly what to do differently—while you still have a chance to fix it.

is it uncomfortable? sometimes. is it effective? absolutely.

our values

🎯 radical honesty

we don't sugarcoat feedback. you can't improve if you don't know what's wrong. brutus tells you the truth, even when it stings.

⚡ real-time action

feedback is worthless if it comes too late. we deliver coaching while you can still do something about it.

📈 continuous improvement

every call is a chance to get better. brutus tracks your progress and celebrates real growth—not participation.

🔒 privacy first

your calls are your business. we encrypt everything and never share your data. period.

the future of sales coaching

we believe every salesperson deserves a world-class coach—not just the ones at big companies with expensive training programs. brutus levels the playing field. whether you're a solo real estate agent or part of a 100-person sales team, you get the same brutally honest, incredibly effective coaching.

ai shouldn't replace human connection in sales. but it can make every salesperson sharper, more aware, and more effective. that's what brutus does.
