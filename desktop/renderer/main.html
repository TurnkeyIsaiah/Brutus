audio setup wizard

set up full conversation capture (both you and your prospect)

step 1
download VB-Cable

VB-Cable is a free virtual audio driver that lets Brutus capture both sides of your conversations.

step 2
install VB-Cable

1. Extract the ZIP file you downloaded

2. Right-click on VBCABLE_Setup_x64.exe

3. Select "Run as administrator"

4. Click "Install Driver"

5. Restart your computer when prompted

step 3
configure windows audio

1. Right-click the speaker icon in your taskbar

2. Click "Sounds" → "Playback" tab

3. Set "CABLE Input" as your default playback device

4. Go to the "Recording" tab

5. Set "CABLE Output" as your default recording device

step 4
route audio to your headphones

Since CABLE Input is now your default playback, you need to hear the audio in your headphones:

1. Open "Sound Control Panel" (search in Windows)

2. Go to "Recording" tab

3. Right-click "CABLE Output" → Properties

4. Click the "Listen" tab

5. Check "Listen to this device"

6. Select your headphones/speakers in the dropdown

7. Click "Apply" then "OK"

step 5
configure your call app

Finally, set your call software (Zoom, Teams, etc.) to use the virtual cable:

Microphone: Select your real microphone

Speakers: Select "CABLE Input"

⚠️ Make sure to do this in your call app's audio settings!

✓ complete
you're all set!

Brutus can now capture both sides of your conversations for complete context and better coaching.
